Laugh Lines


Two blokes are sat in a cafe. The first, Bill, nudges his mate as says: "That tall fella outside in the tracksuit, that's RuPaul isn't it?"

Jim replies: "What? The bloke in the sports tracksuit smoking?"

"Yeah," says Bill. "Will you pop out and get his autograph? I'm a big fan, but I'm too shy to ask."

Jim is not convinced that it's RuPaul, but Bill won't let it go. Eventually, Jim says "If I go and ask him. Will that settle things?"

Jim goes outside and walks up the chap who is still smoking. "Excuse me, mate. Are you RuPaul Charles the famous drag queen and rights activist?"

The man in the tracksuit draws on his cigarette and in a thick northern accent says "What do you think? F*** off with yer"

"Sorry, no offence meant," says Bill and he goes back inside.

"Well," says Jim. "Is it him? What did he say?"

Bill sighs "He told me to eff off"

"Oh," says Jim, "I don't suppose we'll ever know now."

Action Corner (TW: Transphobia)

This week the government is trying to devastate teaching of transgender topics in school. This is quite simply a rehash of Section 28. Be warned, this is going to take a heavy stomach to read.

The department for education is running a consultation on changes to the statutory guidance on what to teach in schools as part of RSHE (Relationships, Sex, and Health Education).

You can fill out this consultation here: https://consult.education.gov.uk/rshe-team/review-of-the-rshe-statutory-guidance/

Let them know how horrifyingly evil this is.

For more information, you can check out Clare Flourish's blog: https://clareflourish.wordpress.com/2024/05/17/relationships-sex-health-education-guidance-review/
