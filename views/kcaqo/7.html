Laugh Lines


Three men are arguing on a beach. One of them happens to kick something in the sand, and another picks it up. Putting aside their differences, they clean it up and *SPOOF!* a genie appears.

The genie looks at the three and says "Well, I'm not entirely sure who rubbed the lamp, so I'm going to give you each a single wish. And you can't wish for more wishes."

The first guy says "Anything we want huh? Okay, as a straight white man I'm sick of being oppressed these days! I want a duplicate Earth just like this one, but with only the straight white folk who think just like me on it. Can I have that?"

"If that's your wish, so be it." says the genie and *SPOOF!* the first man is whisked away.

"My turn!" yells the second guy. "As someone who doesn't believe in this gender nonsense, I want a duplicate world where there's only men and women. None of this gender ideology guff or woke science - just two sexes! I want to live there with all the people who think like me and where real men are rightly in charge."

"If you so desire..." says the genie and *SPOOF!* the second guy vanishes as well.

Finally, the genie looks to the final man, a trans man, holding the lamp. "What's your wish?"

"Hmm," says the young man. "So, given the last two wishes, are you saying there are no more sexists, homophobes, racists, or transphobes on this planet?"

"Yup," replies the genie.

The trans man smiles. "I'll just have a nice cup of tea then."

Action Corner

Register to vote.

https://www.registertovote.service.gov.uk/country-of-residence/

If you haven't heard, there's a general election on the 4th of July.

Even if you haven't decided who to vote for yet, or you haven't vetted all the candidates in your constituency, you should still make sure you have a vote by registering now.

If you don't have a photo ID (or you don't have ID in the correct name), register for a postal vote. You don't need ID to vote by post.
