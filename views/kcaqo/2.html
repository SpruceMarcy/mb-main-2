Featured Song

This week's song is Everybody Wants To Be Loved by Alternative Radio. From 1993, this is a gleefully upbeat piece of music!

Thank you to the anonymous reader who suggested this last week. About this song, they wrote that they were feeling very grateful for all their friendships, and this song embodied that. It certainly brightened my mood, and I hope it'll do the same for all you too!

I think this band qualifies as lesser-known. I certainly struggled to find much information on them, but that just goes to show that obscurity doesn't stop quality.
