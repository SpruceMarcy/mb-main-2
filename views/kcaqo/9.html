Action Corner (TW: Transphobia)

Right before parliament dissolved, the tories enacted emergency legislation banning puberty blockers for transgender people under 18. This affected private care, but only applies to new prescriptions and it only lasts three months.

Needless to say, it is still a devastating attack. Our allies at the Good Law Project have started a legal push-back that picks apart this move entirely.

So what can you do? Well, only really support the GLP. They are asking for donations, so if you can you should either donate or at least spread awareness of this campaign so that others may donate as well.

Here is a link to their landing page for this campaign: https://goodlawproject.org/campaign/stop-the-ban-on-puberty-blockers/

Here is their donation page: https://goodlawproject.org/crowdfunder/trans-pubertyblockers-cf/

And if you are interested, here is a letter they have filed as the first step in the legal action: https://drive.google.com/file/d/1lbSBvs-mtYI31hUPw_8rqxxYLgMBGRry/view/. This details the legal grounds they have, and the avenue they're taking.
