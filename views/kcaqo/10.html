Weekly Puzzle + Showcase

This puzzle was made by Seren, and featured in the 2024 pride puzzler by https://thegriddle.net/. It's called Gender Walk, and the rules are as follows:

Draw a loop that goes through every symbol.

1. The loop cannot branch off or cross itself.

2. Pink cells represent the famed gender fluid, while regular cells represent land. The loop may not go through more than 2 gender fluid cells in a row.

3. Every section of the loop split by the gender fluid must contain at least one symbol, and all those symbols must be the same.

4. Every time the loop goes through the gender fluid, the new section must have a different symbol to adjacent sections.
