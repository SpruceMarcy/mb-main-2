Featured Song

This week's song is Rebel Girl by Bikini Kill. Just like last week's featured song, this is also from 1993. Maybe that was a good year for music?

This punk song is a feminist and lesbian anthem, and is certainly not the only one by Bikini Kill. Credited for backing vocals and guitar on this song is Joan Jett, who you might know from being a part of songs like Cherry Bomb, and I Love Rock & Roll.

Showcase

This week's showcase is the Museum of Liverpool.

If you happen to find yourself in Merseyside, then I recommend you take some time to visit the Museum of Liverpool, on the bank of the river.

Liverpool has a wonderful and rich LGBT history, and their museums know it!

Even if you're not making any plans of the sort, do take some time to look at their digital collection of articles and videos on queer history!

They even have an >LGBTQ+ audio trail made by Matthew Riley and Brendan Geoghegan from the >Bottoming Podcast.
